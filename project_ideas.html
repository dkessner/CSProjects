Video Projections - All School Dance Concert#

You will have the opportunity to work with dancers/choreographers to create video projections for the All School Dance Concert (Processing or p5.js). We will be working on an immersive, interactive visual and audio environment for the audience to experience before the concert.

Visual Novels / Narrative Video Games#
